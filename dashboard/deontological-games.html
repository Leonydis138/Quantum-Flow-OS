Deontological Utility Formulation

Traditional Game Theory assumes players maximize raw Egoistic Utility. Kantian Deontological Game Theory modifies player payoffs:

Payoff = Egoistic + (1.5 * EthicalAlignment) - (1.2 * ExternalCost)

This formulation penalizes selfish actions that impose severe friction or negative externalities on other observer systems.
